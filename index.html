Info & Guide

Shortcuts:
• Ctrl+Shift+S: Toggle Scratchpad
• Ctrl+Shift+H: Toggle History
• Ctrl+Shift+E: Auto-adjust prompt height
• Ctrl+Shift+Q: Cancel generation (when enabled)
• Ctrl+Enter: Generate (when not focused in prompt, or if 'ENTER to send' is off)
• Enter: Generate (when 'ENTER to send' is on and focused in prompt)

Cancel Button: Only enabled when there are images in the queue that can still be cancelled ("Waiting...").
